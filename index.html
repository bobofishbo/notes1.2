Welcome to our short demo!

For our next lesson, we will be showing off our own implementation of a well known encryption
function known as xOR. This encryption function converts a plain text string into bits, randomly
generates a key of bits the same length, and creates a cipher by combining the two pieces of bit
data using exclusive or.

Then the cipher is decrypted with the key to generate the password.
